About

Hi, I’m Anshi Gupta — a curious mind fueled by a passion for technology and data. I’m currently pursuing my Master’s in Data Science at IIIT Lucknow, backed by a strong foundation in Statistics.

My journey into tech began with a simple fascination: the idea that I could make a computer do whatever I imagined. That fascination grew into a love for Machine Learning, AI, and Mlops — where logic, creativity, and real-world impact collide.

I’ve had the opportunity to work on exciting projects, from fine-tuning large language models like LLaMA to building intelligent dashboards and deploying deep learning applications with tools like Python, Streamlit, PyTorch, and Power BI.

Whether I’m building models, coding up dashboards, or diving into new tools, I’m driven by curiosity, purpose, and a hunger to create. Outside of tech, you’ll find me immersed in dancing, exploring YouTube tech deep-dives, or recharging with a good series.

For me, it’s not just about working with data — it’s about creating something meaningful with it.

Projects

Image Captioning

Developed a deep learning model that generates descriptive captions for images using CNN for feature extraction and LSTM for sequence generation. Trained on COCO dataset and evaluated using BLEU score.

RagifyQnA

Built a real-time document Q&A system using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Uses Qdrant and Flasheranker for relevant content retrieval and LLM for answer generation.

Blinkit Sales Dashboard

Created a dynamic Power BI dashboard to visualize Blinkit’s sales trends, order volumes, and regional performance. Enabled stakeholders to identify high-performing products and optimize inventory.

Credit Card Customer Segmentation

Applied K-Means clustering to segment credit card customers based on spending and payment behavior. Helped in identifying customer personas like high spenders, revolvers, and transactors for targeted marketing.

Market Basket Analysis

Coin Oracle

Developed a Streamlit-based app that delivers real-time crypto insights using CoinGecko and Together APIs (LLaMA model). Visualized data using Matplotlib and handled processing with Pandas.
